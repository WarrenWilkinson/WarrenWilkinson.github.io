Growcer in Grande Cache

Growcer is a turn-key hydroponic farm capable of growing leafy greens for the community. If Grande Cache had one, we'd have fresher vegetables at a similar price, and produced locally.

  • Purchasing one costs around $200,000 CAD.
  • We'd need to put it somewhere
  • It requires about 25 hours a week of labor
    • Planting seedlings
    • Harvesting Crops
    • Making deliveries

Who could undertake this project?

I'm investigating several potential avenues for how this thing could be owned and operated:

  1. As a personal private business
  2. As a sub-entity of the grocery store
  3. A community group a. A newly founded one b. Growing Empowered c. The Farmers Market d. Some other group
  4. As a kind of High-School / Library model farm.
  5. As some kind of municipal creation
  6. As a business owned and operated by one of the residential co-ops.
  7. As a project run by a consortium of businesses (e.g. restaurants, grocery stores, farmers market).
