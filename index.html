Grande Cache Fresh Lettuce!

I found a way to create a community garden that grows fresh produce in Grande Cache all year round.

Hydroponic farming in a modular farm makes it easy for everyone to access fresh, local, healthy food year-round – even in the chilliest days of winter.

Growcer is such a modular farm. If Grande Cache had one, we'd have fresher leafy greens at a similar price, but fresher and locally produced.
